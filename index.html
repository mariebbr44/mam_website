About Dr. Marieb

In arrhythmia practice for approximately 25 years, specializing in interventional electrophysiology including ablation (complex atrial arrhythmias including atrial fibrillation, ventricular tachycardia, WPW, AVNRT, PVC's, pacemakers, implantable defibrillators, cardiac resynchronization therapy). Fellow of the Heart Rhythm Society (FHRS), Certified Cardiac Device Specialist, Clinical Director of Electrophysiology Yale University School of Medicine and Yale New Haven Hospital.
